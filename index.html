About me

Beginner web developer with a background in retail and bicycle mechanics. To learn HTML, CSS and Javascript I have run through the CodeCademy tracks of the aforementioned, solved kata on codewars to improve my Javascript algorithm scripting and begun working through FreeCodeCamp's Front End Development certification. I have started to learn Git as well, using GitHub pages to make this webapp. I am in early stages but seeking experience and personal and professional development.
From my experience working at Lush (famed for its impeccable customer service) and teaching people to fix their own bicycles at the not-for-profit project "London Bike Kitchen" I have fantastic communication skills and can work through problem solving with others. I enjoy making complex things work and logically working through problems which is why I am making the transition to web development.
In my spare time I enjoy cycling, particularly ultra-distance racing, placing 10th in The Transatlantic Way - a 2500km single stage bicycle race around Ireland.
